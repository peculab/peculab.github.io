Short-term rental technology · AiraHostPricing recommendation model

Turning Airbnb market evidence into an explainable 30-day pricing plan

For AiraHost, I was responsible for completing the Airbnb price-recommendation model behind practical host-facing pricing guidance.

What the model supports

  • A suggested nightly rate and a day-by-day 30-day pricing plan.
  • A user-pinned Airbnb listing as the primary pricing benchmark.
  • Comparable-listing selection using location, property type, capacity, bedrooms, beds, baths, amenities, and travel dates.
  • Benchmark and local-market comparison, including structural similarity, price distribution, and market offset.
  • Confidence and data-quality explanations showing how many listings were collected, filtered, and used.
  • Supporting indicators such as weekday and weekend averages, estimated occupancy, and estimated monthly revenue.

My practical contribution

I connected the pricing problem, comparable-property evidence, model logic, confidence signals, and report outputs so a host could see both a recommendation and the basis behind it. The result is decision support with visible assumptions—not an unexplained number or an automatic promise of performance.

What this demonstrates: applied regression and recommendation modeling, comparable-market analysis, explainable model output, and end-to-end data-to-product delivery.

Teaching people to direct AI without outsourcing their thinking

My published education work has examined learner-centered educational big-data analytics, ChatGPT and Python API learning, retrieval-augmented Python education, digital humanities with generative AI, and project-based exploration of blockchain and emerging technology.

Why these examples now

The market has moved from “Can AI generate something?” to “Can people govern and repeat the work?”

Current adoption research shows broad organizational use of AI but much earlier deployment of agents. The differentiator is increasingly the operating system around the model: documented workflows, human handoffs, quality standards, evaluation, and responsibility.

That shift matches PECULAB’s strongest applied experience—education that preserves judgment, POCs with explicit evidence, and AI systems designed for human review.
