Why PECULAB exists

Good work needs generosity—and a structure that can sustain it.

I deeply respect educators and community members who give their time freely. I will continue to offer selected public talks, resources, and community activities when I can do so responsibly.

But generosity should remain a choice, not become the system’s default expectation. Public support can widen access; it should not erase personal choice, commitment, or responsibility. When a learning experience asks something real of both sides, the relationship becomes more equal: the educator is accountable for the quality of the work, and the learner is accountable for participating in it.

Free work can be a gift. Paid work can be honest stewardship. Neither should be taken for granted.
