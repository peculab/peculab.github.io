Company position

PecuLab LLC turns academic depth, technical building, and operating discipline into deliverable projects.

PecuLab LLC is led by Yun-Cheng Tsai, Ph.D., an AI and machine learning scholar based in the Seattle area. The company is designed to use the full range of her professional background: computer science, financial technology, quantitative modeling, healthcare and education AI, university teaching, public programming, and digital operations.

The work is practical by design. A project is not complete simply because code runs or a website launches; it needs clear scope, payment logic, operational ownership, understandable AI, user-ready workflows, and documentation that lets partners know what belongs to whom.

Case-by-case collaboration logic

Clear contracting, payment, and settlement lines

FIH is a public initiative and collaboration line that PecuLab LLC may support when a project uses FIH's name, brand, initiative resources, or official partner network.

Projects for other clients, or projects independently developed by PecuLab LLC, are contracted, collected, delivered, and operated through PecuLab LLC unless a separate written agreement says otherwise.

For revenue-sharing collaborations, PecuLab LLC can support transparent settlement records, including receipts, refunds, payment processing fees, agreed direct costs, partner payment requests, and settlement reports.
